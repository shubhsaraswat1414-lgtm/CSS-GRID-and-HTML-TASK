The fr unit stands for fraction. It divides the available space in the container into equal parts.

Three columns are created because grid-template-columns is set to 1fr 1fr 1fr.

When the screen size is reduced, the columns automatically shrink to fit the available space.

The items do not overflow. They adjust their width according to the screen size.

The fr unit is better than px because it is flexible and responsive, while px is fixed and does not adapt to screen size.
