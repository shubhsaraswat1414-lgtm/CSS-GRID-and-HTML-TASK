Grid lines are the vertical and horizontal lines that divide the grid into columns and rows.

The item spans two columns because it starts at grid line 1 and ends at grid line 3.

Other items automatically adjust their position and move to the next available grid cells.

Yes, two items can overlap if they are placed on the same grid area intentionally, but by default Grid avoids overlap.

Column spanning is used in websites for headers, banners, featured sections, and highlighted content.
